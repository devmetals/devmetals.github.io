Established in the year 2001, Dev Metals & Alloys Pvt. Ltd. has been operating in the non-ferrous metal recycling industry for over 15 years. With its manufacturing plant spread over an area of over 60,000 sq. ft., the Company is engaged in the production of alloy ingots of various grades and specifications. Specifically, Dev Metals produces Aluminum, Brass, Zinc, and Copper based alloy ingots.

Dev Metals is owned and operated by entrepreneurs with four generations of experience in non-ferrous metal recycling. The Company is currently led by Mr. Dilipkumar Jain. Mr. Jain has over 30 years of experience in the non-ferrous metal recycling space. During this time, he has also operated an aluminum extrusion plant and a die casting plant engaged in the manufacture of automobile components. As such, Mr. Dilipkumar Jain has a rich experience operating in various steps of the value chain, from sourcing raw materials to production, testing, and quality control of alloy ingots. Exposure to the die casting industry gives Mr. Jain and his team have significant experience and leverage in producing quality alloys that meet various customization needs and quality standards of the die casting industry. With customers in both the automotive and electrical die casting industry, Dev Metals’ leadership ensures the highest standard of quality with respect to hardness, porosity, roughness, homogeneity, and other physical and chemical aspects of an alloy ingot, thereby resulting in negligible levels of rejection of the ingot material at the die casting stage.

The Company is currently focused on catering to the sourcing needs of die casting companies in Pune and Aurangabad. With a current installed capacity of over 600 MT, Dev Metals is poised to double its production capacity over the next 8 months as well as increase its presence to die casting hubs outside of Maharashtra.

Serve the customer by ensuring a seamless supply of customized alloy ingots manufactured using the latest available technology while at the same time maintaining superior product quality and an environmental-friendly production process.

By virtue of operating in the metal recycling space, Dev Metals & Alloys Pvt. Ltd. is able to contribute to the sustainability cause by converting non-ferrous scrap into valuable alloy ingots

The Company continues to serve the customer by constantly diversifying its product and service base while at the same time improving its technical expertise and quality standards by investing in manpower, machinery, and material

Dev Metals & Alloys Pvt. Ltd. has been certified with ISO 9001:2008. The Company routinely reviews and updates its quality control process in order to comply with the requirements of ISO 9001:2008. Further to complying with the ISO 9001:2008 requirements, Dev Metals & Alloys Pvt. Ltd. follows a Total Quality Management (TQM) process wherein it strives to detect, mitigate, and resolve any quality control issues across the various steps of the production process.

Sunalco Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a Non-Ferrous metal recycling unit, producing different grades of Aluminium Alloys as its final product. We form an integral link between the scrap collectors, processors, recyclers, and manufacturing industries; thus completing the circle of Recycling.

Established in the year of 2005, the Company has grown significantly over the past decade, growing its production capacity year after year and becoming one of the market leaders in its industry. Sunalco has installed a production capacity of over 45,000 Metric Tons per annum, making the annual turnover gross over 4500 Million INR in 2015-2016. The production facility which spans over an area of 2,40,000 square feet, is situated in the Industrial belt at Wada, about 65 kilometres from Mumbai and fairly close to the Nhava Sheva Port.

Sunalco has dedicated employees and labourers working throughout the year to ensure that only high quality products are delivered to our clients. Our high quality management system has allowed us to be certified with an ISO 9001:2008 by TUV NORD.

We believe that recycling is not just an environmental tool but an economic tool as well. Recycling and Waste reduction offer direct development opportunities for communities. When collected with skill and care, and upgraded with quality in mind, discarded materials are a resource that can contribute to local revenue, job creation, business expansion, and the local economic base.

The credit rating agency, ICRA has assigned a Stable long-term rating to Sunalco. Sunalco has been rated as BB plus on the long term rating scale of ICRA, just a grade away from a BBB rating which is an investment grade rating.

Sunalco has also been certified with an ISO 9001:2008 by TUV NORD. ISO 9001:2008 specifies requirements for quality management system where an organization

Needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide product that meets customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of the system, including processes for continual improvement of the system and the assurance of conformity to customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.

All requirements of ISO 9001:2008 are generic and are intended to be applicable to all organizations, regardless of type, size and product provided.

The Company has an in-house Spectro Meter for testing the composition of the raw material. Furthermore, the raw material is also visually inspected. Sourcing of the raw material is conducted well in advance of the alloy ingot delivery date in order to maintain the delivery schedule.

The chemical composition of the WIP inventory is constantly tested using the Spectro Meter in order to create a finished ingot that adheres to the chemical composition required by the customer. The furnace is heated to an optimum temperate depending on the nature of the metal / alloy that is to be melted. The Company has a degassing mechanism in place in order to avoid any dross.

Finished Goods are stored separately. Each batch is labelled and color coded in order to differentiate between the finished goods of different grades and specifications. The finished alloy ingot is re-tested using the Spectro Meter before dispatch.

The Company has an in-house transportation fleet in order to dispatch the goods to the customer site. A test report along with (tested) alloy sample is sent with the order of dispatched goods for inspection by the customer.
